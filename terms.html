    4. Warranty Scope

    Hikstorage's products in any of the following circumstances are excluded from Hikstorage's free-of-charge warranty. However, users may choose Out-of-warranty repairs.

    • 4.1 Valid warranty document and original purchase invoice or receipt cannot be shown, and the former serial number label is altered, changed or torn down, there is no serial number or the product model or number on the warranty document is inconsistent with the product.
    • 4.2 The warranty period specified by HIKSTORAGE is exceeded.
    • 4.3 Malfunctions and damages resulting from failure of use, maintenance and storage according to the user manual or the working environment indicated in the user manual.
    • 4.4 Malfunctions or damages resulting from installation, repairs, changes or dis-assembly by the organizations NOT authorized by Hikstorage.
    • 4.5 Damages resulting from accidents or other force majeure.

    5. Limitation of Liability

    If Hikstorage breaches the contract or needs to bear other responsibilities, customers can request Hikstorage to compensate for the losses. However, the compensation liability of Hikstorage is limited to customers actual direct losses and does not assume any responsibility for the following:
    ① the amount exceeding the actual payment price for the product customers purchased;
    ② Any costs and expenses related to obtaining alternative goods, technology, services, or rights;
    ③ Data damage or loss;
    ④ Losses caused by interruptions or delays in use.
    This liability limitation also applies to product sellers and service providers. This is the maximum compensation limit jointly borne by Hikstorage and product sellers and service providers. The above liability limitations apply only to the extent permitted by law and do not apply to liabilities that cannot be excluded or limited by contract or other means in accordance with the law.

    Quality Management & Manufacturing System

    Commitment to Excellence

    Quality management

    Adhering to the principle that "reliability comes first," HIKSEMI has built a rigorous quality management system across R&D and production. The company is certified to ISO 9001:2008 for quality management and ISO 14001:2004 for environmental management, demonstrating its commitment to product excellence and sustainability.

    End-to-end quality control is achieved through advanced 6S management and full supply chain traceability enabled by the SAP system, covering all stages from incoming materials to final delivery. HIKSEMI operates a 1,300-square-meter testing center with over 100 high-precision instruments for thermal, salt spray, vibration, and reliability demonstration testing (RDT), ensuring stable, reliable performance and continuous improvement.

    All products meet applicable regulatory requirements in their target markets and hold key international certifications, including FCC, CE, CCC, as well as RoHS, REACH, and WEEE. Solutions for specialized industries also comply with relevant sector-specific standards and certifications.

    Manufacturing system

    As a global provider of storage products and solutions, HIKSEMI possesses end-to-end capabilities in design, manufacturing, and development from chips to finished goods. The company operates two smart factories in Wuhan and Hangzhou and has established strategic partnerships with leading global wafer foundries as well as packaging and testing providers to strengthen upstream and downstream integration and enhance supply chain resilience.

    The Wuhan facility, operational since 2023, is equipped with advanced packaging and testing lines featuring DB, WB, MD, and FC equipment. It supports mass production of BGA, UFS, and TF products and fulfills 90% of low- to mid-range Flash testing requirements. The Hangzhou plant runs multiple fully automated SMT lines deeply integrated with MES, APS, PDM, and ERP systems, enabling large-scale flexible manufacturing.

    Leveraging an industrial interconnection platform, automated data acquisition, predictive analytics, and other digital technologies, HIKSEMI has built a lean intelligent manufacturing system that tightly combines lean principles, automation, and digitalization for end-to-end operational efficiency.

    In addition, logistics between warehouses and production plants are fully automated through a warehouse management system and intelligent robotics independently developed by Hikvision's Robotics Division.
